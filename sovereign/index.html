Sovereign Portrait List

1817-2025

George III (1817-1820)

First Laureate Head
(Benedetto Pistrucci)

Very few issued in 1819, 10 Copies known

George IV (1820-1830)

Laureate Head (1821-1826)
(Benedetto Pistrucci)

Bare Head (1825-1830)
(William Wyon)

Both portraits were issued alongside eachother briefly.


William IV (1830-1837)

Bare Head (William Wyon)

No Sovereigns Issued in 1834


Victoria (1837-1901)

Young Head (1838-1887)
(William Wyon)

Minted with both the Shield and St. George reverse designs.


Jubilee Head (1887-1893)
(Joseph Edgar Boehm)

1887 had Three Different Sovereign Issues.


Veiled Head (1893-1901)
(Sir Thomas Brock)

The First Sovereigns to Be Struck In Australia.


Edward VII (1901-1910)

Bare Head
(George William de Saulles)

First Canadian Minted Sovereign, With Approximately only 640 Coins Minted (1908-C Sovereign).


George V (1910-1936)

Bare Head
(Edgar Bertram Mackennal)

No 1910 Edward VI Sovereigns exist, 1910 Consisted of only Edward VII Sovereigns.


George VI (1936-1952)

Bare Head
(T.H. Paget)

Only issued in 1937 with a Proof mintage of 5500, Additionally, 1 specimen struck as Matte Proof for The King's personal collection.


Elizabeth II (1952-2022)

First Portrait (1953-1967)

Proof sovereigns with the portrait were struck in 1953, but no further issues were produced until 1957..


Second Portrait (1968-1984)
(Arnold Machin)

Did not appear on any sovereigns until 1974, as the royal mint suspended sovereign production temporary after decimalisation


Third Portrait (1985-1997)
(Raphael Maklouf)

Did not appear on any Sovereign issues until 1974, as The Royal Mint had temporarily suspended Sovereign production after the decimalisation of UK currency.


Fourth Portrait (1998-2015)
(Ian Rank-Broadley)

Final issue year (2015) featured both the 4th and 5th portrait strikes in the same year.


Fifth Portrait (2015-2022)
(Jody Clark)

2021 Issue had the "95" Privy Mark



Charles III (2022-2025)

Standard Portrait (2022-Present)
(Martin Jennings)

2023 strike was only available as a "Strike on The Day" edition on 14/11/2023


Crowned Portrait (2023-Present)
(Martin Jennings)

Portrait Only used on Collector's coins, None issued for circulation
